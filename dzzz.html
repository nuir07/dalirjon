Formula 1

Formula 1 (F1) is the highest class of international motorsport. It features the fastest and most advanced racing cars in the world, driven by the best drivers. Each season consists of a series of races, called Grands Prix, held on circuits and city streets across the globe.


Teams such as Ferrari, Mercedes, Red Bull, and McLaren compete not only for victories but also to develop cutting-edge technology that often influences road cars. F1 is known for its speed, precision, and strategy — where every second counts and teamwork plays a crucial role.

Charles Leclerc

Charles Leclerc is a Formula 1 driver from Monaco, born on October 16, 1997. He races for Scuderia Ferrari, one of the most famous teams in motorsport history. Leclerc joined Ferrari in 2019 and quickly became known for his incredible speed, determination, and qualifying performances.


Before reaching F1, Leclerc won the GP3 Series in 2016 and the Formula 2 Championship in 2017. His driving style is aggressive yet precise, and hes admired for his focus and humility. Fans around the world see him as one of the most talented and promising drivers on the grid, often fighting for podiums and victories.

Max Verstappen

Max Verstappen is a Formula 1 driver from the Netherlands, born on September 30, 1997. He races for Red Bull Racing and is one of the most successful drivers in modern F1 history. Verstappen made his debut in 2015 at just 17 years old, becoming the youngest driver ever to compete in Formula 1.


He won his first World Championship in 2021 after a dramatic season finale, and went on to dominate the 2022 and 2023 seasons, winning multiple races and breaking several records. Known for his aggressive yet controlled driving style, Max is praised for his consistency, racecraft, and mental strength.

Today, Verstappen is considered one of the greatest talents in Formula 1, representing a new era of speed and precision.

Lewis Hamilton

Lewis Hamilton is a British Formula 1 driver, born on January 7, 1985. He races for Mercedes-AMG Petronas Formula One Team and is widely regarded as one of the greatest drivers in the history of the sport.


Hamilton made his F1 debut in 2007 and won his first World Championship in 2008 with McLaren. After joining Mercedes in 2013, he dominated the sport, winning multiple titles between 2014 and 2020, and matching Michael Schumachers record of seven World Championships.

Known for his smooth driving style, exceptional racecraft, and strong mindset, Lewis has also become a global icon beyond racing — advocating for diversity, equality, and environmental awareness.
